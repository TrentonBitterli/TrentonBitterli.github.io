Forest Exploration

GIMM 400 Multiplayer Game Development



Description

For one of my GIMM projects we were assigne to make a group game using unreal engine. We were assigned a client that was legally blind, and tasked to make a game for her to play that included the use of an accessibility controller so she could play the game without trouble or assistance. We decided to go with a forest exploration game since she loves open world exploration games. We created three different prototype levels: One where you have to avoid enemy lumberjack ai, one where you have to match animal sounds with animal models, And one where you have to collect a key and then run from an enemy lumberjack who chases you through a firey path in the forest. The game is designed for two players, one who can see but not hear, and one who can hear but not see.

Skills Used

  • Unreal Engine Game Mechanics
  • Blueprints
  • Problem Solving 'Working with a legally blind client'
